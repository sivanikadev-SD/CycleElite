Coaching FAQs

How often do I communicate with my coach?
On the Elite plan, you have unlimited messaging and bi-weekly 1-on-1 video calls. The Pro plan includes weekly message check-ins and performance review comments on your key workouts.
Can I change my coach if I'm not satisfied?
Absolutely. We offer a "Perfect Match Guarantee". If for any reason your current coach isn't the right fit, our team will personally assist you in transitioning to a new coach at no additional cost.
