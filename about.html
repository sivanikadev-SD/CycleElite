The History

How It All Began

In 2018, our founders realized that amateur cyclists were training completely blind. While World Tour teams had access to millions of data points, metabolic testing, and AI-driven periodization, the everyday athlete was left guessing.

We set out to democratize elite performance. By combining deep-learning algorithms with thousands of hours of real-world coach feedback, CycleElite was born. Today, we process over 50,000 workouts daily, constantly refining the platform to help you break your personal records.
