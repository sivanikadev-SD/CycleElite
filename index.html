FAQs

Commonly Asked

How does the AI training adjustment work?
Our LoadDelta engine analyzes your HRV, sleep data, and past 48 hours of TSS (Training Stress Score). If it detects excessive fatigue or a "supercompensation" window, it automatically scales your upcoming session intensity by up to 15%.
What sensors can I connect?
CycleElite connects with all ANT+ and Bluetooth Smart sensors including Power Meters (dual-sided supported), Heart Rate Monitors, Cadence sensors, and Core body temperature sensors.
Can I switch plans mid-way through a block?
Yes, you can transition between Beginner, Intermediate, and Pro tracks at any time. The system will recalculate your TSS targets based on your current 6-week CTL.
Do I need a power meter to use CycleElite?
While a power meter unlocks our most advanced features like FTP progression and precise TSS targeting, you can still use our platform with a heart rate monitor to get estimated load and recovery metrics.
