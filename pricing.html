Common Questions

Everything You Need to Know

Can I cancel my subscription anytime?
Yes, CycleElite operates on a month-to-month basis. You can cancel your subscription at any time through your dashboard settings with no hidden fees or penalties.
Which devices are compatible with the platform?
We support all major cycling computers and wearable devices, including Garmin, Wahoo, Polar, Suunto, and COROS. We also sync directly with Strava and TrainingPeaks.
How do I get matched with a coach?
Once you sign up for an Elite or Pro plan, you'll complete a brief questionnaire about your goals and experience. Our matching algorithm then recommends three coaches that best fit your profile for you to choose from.
