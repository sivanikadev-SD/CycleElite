Training Framework

The CycleElite Curriculum

Our plans are built on the 'LoadDelta' methodology, ensuring every watt of effort results in measurable physiological adaptation.

Phase 1: Foundation

Weeks 1-4 focus on neuromuscular coordination and aerobic base expansion. We prioritize posture and metabolic efficiency over raw intensity.

Key Drills
  • • High-cadence spin-ups
  • • Unilateral pedal strokes
  • • Z2 endurance saturation

Phase 2: Power Density

Weeks 5-8 introduce 'Sweet Spot' protocols and FTP-specific blocks. These sessions increase your ability to sustain high power for extended periods.

Key Drills
  • • 2x20min FTP Intervals
  • • Over-Under threshold sets
  • • Sustained climbing simulations

Phase 3: Peak & Taper

Weeks 9-12 focus on event-specific intensity and volume reduction. We sharpen your top-end speed while shedding tactical fatigue.

Key Drills
  • • High-torque sprint starts
  • • V02 max micro-intervals
  • • Dynamic race simulations

Training FAQs

Common Training Questions

What if I miss a workout session?
Life happens! Our LoadDelta algorithm automatically adjusts your upcoming sessions based on missed workouts or unexpected fatigue. Just log the reason and your plan will adapt the next time you sync.
Can I switch plans mid-way through a block?
Yes, you can transition between Beginner, Intermediate, and Pro tracks at any time. The system will recalculate your TSS targets based on your current 6-week CTL (Chronic Training Load).
